Connect Your Strava Account

This app will fetch all your Strava data (activities, zones, gear, routes) so you can export it for AI analysis.

Get this from Strava API Settings

What happens next?

  1. You'll be redirected to Strava to authorize this app
  2. After authorization, your data will be fetched automatically
  3. Once complete, you can export everything as a JSON file
  4. Use the JSON file with any AI chatbot for training planning and analysis

Permissions Required

  • Read your public profile
  • Read all your activities (including private ones)
  • Read your gear and equipment
  • Read your routes
